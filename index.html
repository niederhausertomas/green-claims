Green Claims Cheat Sheet for Fashion

Avoid greenwashing following this practical guide to assess whether your green claims follow best communication practices, EU regulations, and consumer expectations.

Answer these key questions according to the criteria your claim meets, and check your score at the end!
1. Is your environmental claim based on recognized scientific research? Yes No
2. Have you assessed the impacts using a life-cycle perspective? Yes No
3. Have you indicated whether the claim applies to the whole product or just part of it? Yes No
4. Are you using accurate, primary, or secondary data to support your claims? Yes No
5. Do you mention the impact indicator being referenced in your claim? Yes No
6. Do you reference scientifically based impact indicators (e.g., water scarcity, global warming)? Yes No
7. Do you maintain the unit of measurement and the 'eq' (e.g., CO₂ eq)? Yes No
8. Are you avoiding vague or misleading terms like "eco-friendly"? Yes No
9. If using terms with assumed meanings (e.g., "organic," "biodegradable"), are they properly justified? Yes No No aplicable
10. Can your claims be easily understood by consumers, avoiding technical or confusing language? Yes No
11. Is your claim clearly more than just meeting legal requirements or standard industry practices? Yes No
12. Has your claim been verified by a third party, not just self-verified? Yes No
13. Have you provided access to substantiating evidence? Yes No
14. If you're using sustainability-related visuals, do they accurately reflect reality? Yes No No aplicable
15. Are you ensuring one positive aspect doesn't mask significant negative impacts? Yes No
16. If you're using aggregated environmental scores, is it approved by the EU? Yes No No aplicable
17. If comparing items, are you comparing only with products that serve the same function? Yes No No aplicable
18. If comparing items or previous assessments, are they fact-based, significant, and verifiable? Yes No No aplicable
19. If you claim carbon neutrality, have you explained if it's via cuts or offsets? Yes No No aplicable
20. If you claim carbon neutrality, have you provided details on your offset programs? Yes No No aplicable

Green claims score interpretation

Leading practice

Score: 15 to 20
Great job! You're communicating responsibly and with transparency.

Developing practice

Score: 10 to 14
You’re on the right track. Improve clarity and add evidence where possible.

At risk of greenwashing

Score: 0 to 9
Review your claims. You may be at risk of non-compliance or misleading messaging.
