Green Claims Cheat Sheet for Fashion

Avoid greenwashing following this practical guide to assess whether your green claims follow best communication practices, EU regulations, and consumer expectations.

Answer these key questions according to the criteria your claim meets, and check your score at the end!

3. Have you indicated whether the claim applies to the whole product or just part of it? Yes No
4. Are you using accurate, primary, or secondary data to support your claims? Yes No
5. Do you mention the impact indicator being referenced in your claim? Yes No

7. Do you maintain the unit of measurement and the 'eq' (e.g., CO₂ eq)? Yes No

11. Is your claim clearly more than just meeting legal requirements or standard industry practices? Yes No
12. Has your claim been verified by a third party, not just self-verified? Yes No

15. Are you ensuring one positive aspect doesn't mask significant negative impacts? Yes No
